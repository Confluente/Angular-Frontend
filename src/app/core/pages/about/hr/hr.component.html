The Honors Room

The Honors Room is located on the ground floor of MetaForum, 0.094. It is filled with a creative, relaxing working atmosphere. People can eat wherever they can, there is a water boiler for coffee and tea and a couch to relax. And when one needs to free his or her mind, there are table tennis bats available to use outside.

Activities such as quizzes, skill training, and the general member meetings also take place in this room. The Honors Room is the place to be for all the Honors students, it is the place to be part of the community.
